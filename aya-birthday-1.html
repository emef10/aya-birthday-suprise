Things I know about you

🦋
It's definitely NOT a rabbit
That symbol in your name? A butterfly. A 100% butterfly. Not a rabbit with long ears and chubby cheeks. You've made this very clear, multiple times, with great patience.
📺
K-drama & Turkish series enthusiast
From Burak Deniz to Nidhal Saadi, your taste in actors is... exceptional. (Better than Burak, reportedly.)
🎨
Secret artist who burns her work
You draw to express yourself, then burn the evidence. Only the rarest Aya drawings survive. This makes them priceless.
💪
Gym = therapy
Not for aesthetics — for releasing negative energy. Which means somewhere in Tunisia there's a very relieved punching bag.
☮️
Creates happiness from nothing
No special circumstances needed. You find calm in routine, and from that calm — you build your own joy. That's rarer than most people know.
🌷
Tulips over apologies
When you're angry, tulips calm you down. And chocolate. And apparently the right company — though you won't admit that easily.
